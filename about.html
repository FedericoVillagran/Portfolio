I’m Federico, an Uruguayan designer currently living in Madrid, Spain.

Before becoming a designer I was a barista, chef, waitress, brand strategic planner, marketing analyst, gardener, carpenter and accountant... I was founder of a specialty coffee shop called Santana Cafe, and got to wear many hats.

I’ve always been attracted to design and to the creation process of things. I find satisfaction in pouring my energy into the construction of something (tangible or digital) that is useful, helpful, or inspiring.

When I was little I was very atracted to painting and drawings. More as a teenager I founded seduced by carpentry and construction.

I’m focusing my energy now in creating products that will have a positive environmental impact.
